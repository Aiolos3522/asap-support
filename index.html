ASAP

ASAP is a boat monitoring dashboard designed for sailors who want quick access to essential onboard information.

Features

  • Battery monitoring
  • Solar production monitoring
  • Tank level monitoring
  • Weather observations
  • Sea level information
  • Environmental monitoring

Privacy Policy

ASAP may use device location services to provide weather and environmental information relevant to the vessel location.

Location information is used only for application functionality and is not sold to third parties.

ASAP does not collect personal information for advertising purposes.

Data displayed in the application is intended solely for boat monitoring and operational purposes.
